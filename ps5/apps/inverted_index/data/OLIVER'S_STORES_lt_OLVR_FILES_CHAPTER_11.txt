OLIVER'S STORES &lt;OLVR> FILES CHAPTER 11
Oliver's Stores Inc said it has decided to reorganize under Chapter 11 of the 
federal bankruptcy laws and will file a petition by the end of this week.     
The company said it has failed to reach agreement with primary lenders 
Manufacturers Hanover Corp &lt;MHC> and Midlantic Corp &lt;MIDL> on a debt 
restructuring.     It said Manufacturers has declared the company in default 
and demanded repayment of about six mln dlrs in debt.  Reuter &#3;
