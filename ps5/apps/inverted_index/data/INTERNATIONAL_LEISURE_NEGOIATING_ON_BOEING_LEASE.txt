INTERNATIONAL LEISURE NEGOIATING ON BOEING LEASE
&lt;International Leisure Group Plc> said its &lt;Air Europe> subsidiary was in 
advanced negotiations with manufacturers and banks for the lease of 10 
&lt;Boeing Co> and 13 &lt;Rolls-Royce Ltd> engines.     The deal would be for 
the lease, with purchase options, of five Boeing 757-200 planes and five 
737-400s. The Rolls-Royce RB211-535E4 engines would be fitted to the Boeing 
757s.     Delivery would be in 1988 and 1989.  REUTER &#3;
